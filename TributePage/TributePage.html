James Gosling

The creator of Java

James Gosling was part of Sun Microsystems; which developed Java

Intersting Facts of Gosling

  • Credited with inventing the Java programming lanuage in 1994
  • James Gosling has a Bachelor of Science from the University of Calgary. and an M.A dn Ph.d from Carnegie Mellon University.
  • Worth a version of Emcacs called Gosling Emacs (Gosmacs).
  • He built a multi-processor version of Unix for a 16-way computer system while in University.

Career:

  1. Worked at Sun Microsystems for 26 years (1984 to 2010).
  2. Workd at Google from 2011 to 2016.
  3. Where he left to work at Liquid Robotics a start up. Until it got acquired by Boeing in 2016.
  4. As of May 2017 Gosling is working at Amazon Web Services

Awards:

  1. 2002: he was awarded The Economist Innovation Award.
  2. 2002: he was awarded The Flame Award USENIX Lifetime Achievement Award.
  3. 2007: he was made an Officer of the Order of Canada.
  4. 2013: he became a fellow of the Association for Computing Machinery.
  5. 2015: awarded IEEE John von Neumann Medal.
